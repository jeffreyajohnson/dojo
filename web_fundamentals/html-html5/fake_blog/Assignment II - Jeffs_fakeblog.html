The Word nothing

According to Merriam-Webster's Dictionary, here is the definition of the word nothing.

  1. : not any thing : no thing <leaves nothing to the imagination>
  2. : no part
  3. : one of no interest, value, or consequence <they mean nothing to me>

    4. - nothing doing
    : by no means : definitely no

    - nothing for it
    : no alternative <nothing for it but to start over>

The Town Nothing

Did you know that there is a town in Arizona called "Nothing"?

Nothing, AZ Google Map
County Mohave County
Settled 1977
Elevation 3,268 ft (996 m)
Population (2008) 0
Coordinates: 34°28′47″N 113°20′7″W

It also prides itself on being the "rattlesnake capital of Arizona." It's basically gone now - nothing but a ghost town (no pun intended). All that's there is an old gas station and garage.

Now you would think a ghost town in Arizona would be a relic from the old west, but it's not! This town was established in 1977 - less than 40 years ago! For me, 1977 is not all that long ago; that was the year I graduated high school. Oh gawd! Does that mean I'm an old relic too? Are the desert winds just waiting to wisp me away????
